Core Competencies

What I Do

From managing inboxes to resolving complex customer issues — here is what I bring to every role.

📧

Email & Inbox Management

I manage high-volume inboxes end-to-end — sorting, prioritising, drafting replies, and maintaining inbox zero. I set up filters, labels, and auto-responses so no message ever slips through the cracks. Clients typically see a 70% reduction in inbox backlog within the first month.

💬

Live Chat & Customer Support

I handle real-time customer queries across chat platforms and help desks with speed, empathy, and professionalism. Whether it is answering product questions, resolving complaints, or escalating issues — I keep customers satisfied and response times consistently low.

📅

Calendar & Scheduling

I coordinate complex schedules across multiple time zones — booking meetings, setting reminders, preventing double-bookings, and keeping executives' calendars clean and conflict-free. I use Calendly and Google Calendar to create seamless booking experiences for clients and their teams.

🎫

Ticket & Issue Resolution

I manage support ticket queues from first contact to resolution. I log, categorise, prioritise, and follow up on every ticket ensuring nothing falls through. With a 98% first-contact resolution rate, I help businesses reduce repeat contacts and improve overall support quality.

📋

Administrative & Data Tasks

I handle the behind-the-scenes work that keeps businesses moving — data entry, document organisation, report preparation, spreadsheet management, and file archiving. I build systems that make information easy to find, track, and act on at any time.

🤝

CRM & Client Relationship Support

I update and maintain CRM records, track leads and pipelines, send follow-up communications, and ensure no client relationship is neglected. I work with HubSpot and Pipedrive to keep sales and support teams fully aligned and informed at every stage.

📞

Virtual Meeting & Communication Support

I organise and facilitate virtual meetings — sending invites, preparing agendas, taking notes, and following up with action items. I ensure every meeting is productive and that communication between teams, clients, and stakeholders stays clear and consistent.

📁

Document & File Management

I design and maintain organised cloud filing systems using Google Drive, ensuring documents are properly named, version-controlled, and accessible to the right people. I also prepare reports, proposals, and summaries on behalf of busy executives and teams.
